ABOUT

Aden Consultancy is a recruitment agency that provides recruitment services in technology, sales, and marketing functions. Based in London, we offer UK-wide support to our clients. Aden Consultancy aims to establish long-term and trusting relationships with both business partners and candidates. We are always here to support your business whether you are at the beginning of your journey as a start-up or you are a leading company in your industry. We understand selecting the right talent is crucial for companies and we care about candidate experience.

Aden Consultancy, while exerting an effort to reach a fast and quality result with a perfectionist approach, conducts its business processes adhering to high ethical standards, honesty and confidentiality principles.

Services for the Employers

1Recruitment
We offer you a comprehensive search to find and attract best candidates for various job functions. Besides conducting the end to end recruitment process, we can also help you in partial tasks such as CV sourcing, phone interviewing, background checks. We are currently running 10% introductory offer!
2Talent Mapping
Talent maps lay the groundwork for competitive hiring. It provides you the data to attract the right people and the facts you need to budget for an appropriate salary and benefit package for the position. When you evaluate the market for talent, you will see who your competitors employ and their job descriptions. By targeting the right areas and the right people, you make better use of time and resources with a highly efficient process.
3Gift Boxes for the Employees
As you can imagine, happy people are more productive in business! Make your employees feel invaluable and ensure their commitment with your thoughtful presents. We deliver customised gift boxes for your corporation. For available packages, please contact us.

Start Whistle

You inform us that you have a position to be filled at your company and request our support.

Kick-off

You tell us the candidate profile you are looking for. We get the job briefing from you and fully understand your needs. We discuss and define required qualifications and skills together.

Attack

We immediately publish job adverts on multiple platforms, meanwhile we proactively approach the passive candidates. After screening all potential candidates and conducting detailed interviews, we narrow the list down to the most suitable candidates for you.

Thrust

You meet the shortlisted candidates and provide us with some feedback. We also receive feedback from the candidates which are very valuable for the hiring process.

Gooaaalll!

Once you decide on a candidate you want for your team, we forward your formal job offer to your chosen candidate.
